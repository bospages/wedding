Pierré & Tasmin

We are both very happy and excited for you to share our special day with us!

We met in December 2013 at Magalies Park. There was an immediate chemistry between us. Needless to say, a few dates followed in the weeks thereafter. And so our story began… In the month of October 2014 we had our first trip together to the Kruger National Park. Which since then, after many repeat visits, has become our special place.

Pierré: “ It was on one of these visits that I decided to surprise Tasmin by taking us to the Pafuri Private Game Lodge. Little did she know that this was not the only surprise planned for this trip…”

Tasmin: “We had just come back from an amazing early morning game drive. We were alone sitting on the deck, staring out over the vast bush with only the sounds of a few zebra’s grazing at the river bank below. For those of you that know Pierré well, will know that he is a man of few words when it comes to his deepest feelings. However, at this very moment he shared all that was in his heart. I won’t ever forget that moment, when Pierré got down on one knee and asked me to marry him. Needless to say, I said yes!”
